GETNS ECA Policies

Enrollment in our extra curricular activities is subject to the following policies:

1. All students must submit a completed application form to enroll in any activity.

2. Attendance is mandatory for all scheduled sessions. If a student is unable to attend, they must inform the activity leader in advance.

3. Students are expected to demonstrate respect and cooperation during all activities.

4. The school reserves the right to remove any student from an activity for inappropriate behavior.

5. Parents/guardians must sign a consent form for their child to participate in any off-campus activities.

6. GDPR and ECA policies must be consented to when filling out the application form
